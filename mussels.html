Mussels with Chorizo

Description

You will love these mussels with chorizo, a twist on tradition. The rustic, tomato-based broth combined with the amazing flavors from the chorizo will please your taste buds. Some farm-raised mussels may come already prepared, but usually, you will have to soak, scrub, and debeard them. Here's everything you need to know about How to Clean Mussels.

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 10 mins

Total time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh mussels
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1 poblano pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 225g diced chorizo
  • 1 tablespoon panca pepper paste
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 150ml seafood stock or chicken stock

Steps

  1. Soak mussels for 10 mins, clean, and debeard mussels.
  2. Heat oil in a Dutch oven or cast iron skillet. Add shallots, poblano peppers, and garlic. Stir until fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Add chorizo; cook and stir until browned and crumbly. 5 to 7 minutes. Stir in panca pepper paste. Add roasted tomatoes and stock. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and bring broth to a simmer. Add mussels; cover and cook until shells open, about 5 minutes.
